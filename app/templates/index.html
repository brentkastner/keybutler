How It Works

1

Create a Vault

Store your diceware keyphrase securely in a vault. Your keyphrase will be split into multiple shares using Shamir's Secret Sharing.

2

Add Beneficiaries

Designate who should receive access to your keys. Add their contact information and public key.

3

Configure Dead Man's Switch

Set how often you need to check in. If you miss check-ins, the switch will activate after warning periods.

4

Automatic Access

If the switch is triggered, your beneficiaries can request access to the vault and retrieve the keys.
